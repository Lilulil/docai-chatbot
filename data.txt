ACME Cloud Storage — Frequently Asked Questions
================================================

ABOUT ACME CLOUD
ACME Cloud is a secure online file storage and backup service. Founded in 2019,
we help individuals and small businesses store, sync, and share files across all
their devices. Our servers are located in the United States and the European Union.

PRICING PLANS
- Free plan: 5 GB of storage, no cost, forever. Includes file sync on up to 2 devices.
- Personal plan: $8 per month for 500 GB of storage and sync on unlimited devices.
- Business plan: $18 per user per month for 2 TB of storage per user, admin controls,
  and priority support.
All paid plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time. We offer a
14-day free trial on both the Personal and Business plans.

SUPPORTED FILE TYPES
ACME Cloud supports all file types. There is no restriction on file extensions.
The maximum size for a single file upload is 5 GB on the Free plan and 50 GB on
paid plans.

SECURITY
All files are encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256.
Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available on all plans, including Free.
We never sell your data to third parties.

FILE SHARING
You can share any file or folder by generating a secure link. Links can be set to
expire after a chosen number of days, and you can add a password for extra
protection. Business plan users can also set view-only or edit permissions per
person.

DATA RECOVERY
Deleted files are kept in the Trash for 30 days on the Free plan and 180 days on
paid plans. During this window you can restore them with one click. After the
retention window, files are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

SUPPORT
Free plan users get email support with a 48-hour response time. Personal plan
users get email support with a 24-hour response time. Business plan users get
priority support with a 4-hour response time, plus live chat during business
hours (9am–6pm US Eastern, Monday to Friday).

CANCELLATION AND REFUNDS
You can cancel your subscription at any time from Account Settings. If you cancel,
your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. We offer a
full refund if you cancel within 14 days of your first payment.
